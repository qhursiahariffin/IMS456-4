Kasmarini attended Sekolah Rendah Kebangsaan Methodist Pelabuhan Klang, Selangor and Sekolah Agama Rakyat Kampung Kastam, Selangor for her primary education. She enrolls in Sekolah Agama Menengah Tinggi Hisamuddin Kampung Jawa, Klang, Selangor for her secondary education, but she decides to change schools because she does not want to stay in a hostel, so she transfers to Sekolah Menengah Kebangsaan (P) Raja Zarina, Pelabuhan Klang, Selangor. During her form 4 time, she changed schools where she went to a school in Gombak.


Kasmarini earned a Diploma in Information Management from the Universiti Teknologi MARA branch in Kelantan in 2004, and went on to complete a Bachelor of Science in Information Studies (HONS)(LIBRARY AND INFORMATION MANAGEMENT) First Class (IJAZAH SARJANA MUDA (KEPUJIAN) from the Universiti Teknologi MARA branch in Shah Alam. After completing her degree, she was offered a position as a Lecturer in the Universiti Teknologi MARA branch in Shah Alam, where she would study for a year and a half for her Master in Library Science (SARJANA (MASTER)).


In present day, Kasmarini currently holds the position as Senior Lecturer in UiTM Puncak Perdana.
